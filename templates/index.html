What is Osteoporosis?

Osteoporosis is a medical condition characterized by the weakening of bones, making them fragile and more prone to fractures. It occurs when the density and quality of bone are reduced, leading to a higher risk of fractures, especially in the hip, spine, and wrist. Osteoporosis often progresses silently without noticeable symptoms until a fracture occurs.

Preprocessing Steps

  1. Converted all gray scale (1 channel) images to RGB (3 channel) because it is required for transfer learning model
  2. Resized all images to 300 x 300 pixels
  3. Added some normal and osteoporosis images from another dataset to address class imbalance
  4. To solve the imbalance issue and increase the dataset size, applied data augmentation
